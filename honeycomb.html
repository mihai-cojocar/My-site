The Extraction Timeline

Our non-invasive 3-stage process designed to guarantee optimal quality while keeping the hive safe.

01

Cap Removal

We carefully slice the delicate outer beeswax seal manually using warm uncapping tools, avoiding any damage to the inner structural hex-cells.

02

Centrifugal Spin

Frames are spun inside low-RPM stainless steel extractors. Centrifugal force gently pulls the raw liquid honey out without generating heat.

03

Gravity Settling

The honey rests in tanks for 14 days. Wax particles naturally float to the top, ensuring crystal-clear separation without destroying organic nutrients.
